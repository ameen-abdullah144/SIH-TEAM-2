About the Solution

The DWLR monitoring system processes data from 14,000 DWLRs, identifying anomalies such as abnormal data, no data, and low battery levels. With real-time monitoring and anomaly detection, it ensures the accuracy and consistency of ground water measurements, helping officials take immediate corrective actions.
